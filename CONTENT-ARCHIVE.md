## About MoT+++ {#about}

**Description:**
We are an independent artist-run contemporary art space in Saigon, Vietnam. Our fluid and responsive approach to programming prioritizes artists by acknowledging the creative process as often non-linear and divergent, encouraging them to experiment with the boundaries of their practice.

Born as a project space in 2015, our list of +1s activities is expanding as the space continues to grow and evolve its artistic and collaborative practices.

In 2018 we co-founded A. Farm international art residency.

**Location (old WordPress):** Floor 6, Amanaki Thao Dien Hotel, 10 Nguyễn Đăng Giai, Thảo Điền, Thủ Đức, Hồ Chí Minh
**Hours (old WordPress):** Wednesday–Friday, 11am–4pm & by appointment
**Email:** motplusplusplus@gmail.com
**Instagram:** @motplusplusplus

## +1 Trash ♻ ? {#trash}

**Page URL:** https://motplusplusplus.com/1-trash-%e2%99%bb/

**Tagline (EN):** "collecting trash is always an act to make the world a better place"
**Tagline (VI):** "thu gom rác luôn là một hành vi làm đẹp cho đời"

### Program Description

MoT+++'s former office was converted into an art warehouse where local and international artists consign favorite works. Works are displayed casually in physical and virtual formats indefinitely.

**Format — Saigon Domaine Warehouse (2020):**
- Ongoing salon series
- Every Tuesday and Friday, 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Casual venue for examining artworks from past exhibitions
- Combines recycling of artworks and ideas with art rediscovery

**Virtual trash ♻ ?** — Returning soon

### Artist Quote
**Cam Xanh:**
> "art is among few virtues of human trash"
> "nghệ thuật là một trong số ít rác rưởi có đạo đức của loài người"
