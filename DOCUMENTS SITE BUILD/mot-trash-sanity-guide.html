+1 trash — adding a work

Editor guide  ·  motplusplus.sanity.studio

Log in

Go to motplusplus.sanity.studio and sign in with your Google account. You should land on the Studio dashboard.

Add a new work

1In the left sidebar, click +1 Trash Item.
2Click the pencil / compose icon at the top of the list (or the New document button) to create a new entry.
3Fill in the fields. All fields are optional except artist name. Upload at least one image — the first image is used as the main display image.
4If the work is also placed in +1 museum by any other name, use the Linked museum location field to connect it. Search by the work title or artist name and select the matching location. This creates the "view on museum map" button automatically.
5When ready to publish, click the Publish button in the bottom-right corner. The work will appear on the site within a few minutes.

Hide or remove a work

To temporarily hide a work without deleting it, uncheck Active (show on page) and republish. To delete permanently, open the document, click the three-dot menu (⋯) at the top right, and select Delete.

Edit an existing work

Click any entry in the +1 Trash Item list to open it, make your changes, and click Publish. Unpublished changes are saved as drafts automatically — they won't appear on the live site until published.
